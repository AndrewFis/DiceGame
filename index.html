How it plays

  1. Black rolls five dice and plays for odd sums.
  2. White rolls five dice and plays for even sums.
  3. Players alternate placing one rolled die into the 3x3 grid.
  4. Each completed row, column, or diagonal is worth one point.
  5. The second player will have one die left unplayed.
